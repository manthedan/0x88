    Quick docs

    client only
    • Drag a legal move; Tiny Leela replies automatically.
    • Use the intro card to choose side, clock, and Normal or Hand & Brain mode.
    • Use Take back, u, or Backspace to undo your last turn; Tiny Leela’s reply is removed too when available.
    • Hand & Brain modes: You are Hand = Tiny Leela names a piece; You are Brain = you name a piece and Tiny Leela chooses the move. Local modes let both humans alternate with AI Brain or AI Hand.
    • Use ?side=black to default the intro to Black.
    • Use ?view=analysis to boot into analysis mode without book setup.
    • Pick visits from the Model panel, or use ?visits=512&batch=16.
    • Use adaptive neural-eval budgeting with ?budgetMode=neural&visits=512; cache hits are free, with maxVisitsMultiplier capping extra visits.
    • Playable search defaults to searchMode=monty_lc0both. Use the Search dropdown or URL values classic, monty, monty_lc0both, widen, widen_lc0both, or puct_lc0both.
    • Tiny Leela PV is on by default; disable with ?pv=0, or tune with pvDepth and pvSelector=visits|q|puct.
    • Opening book defaults to 4 plies; try ?bookPlies=8 for deeper book help, or ?opening=start to disable it.
